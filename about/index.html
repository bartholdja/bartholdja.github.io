Hello and welcome! My name is Julia Barthold and I am a postdoc at the University of Southern Denmark. While my background is in biology and mathematical demography, my interests span from positive psychology to big data computing. As a result my work intersects human demography, behaviour, and the development of statistical tools. I also build mathematical representations of real-world systems to answer "what would be if" questions.

In this space I share things, thoughts, and tools--side by side.
